OUR LOVE STORY

Growing up, I always loved walking by the windows of bridal shops. Looking at all the beautiful gowns, I dreamt about the day when I would be the one walking down the aisle. I remember drawing my own wedding gown - I always pictured it to be a real princess dress with lots of tulle layers. However, when that day finally come years later, I wanted something different. Yes, I still wanted a gown in which I would feel like a princess, but I wanted it to be much more minimalistic. I started searching for dresses, but no matter how hard I tried, I simply just could not find a dress that suited my taste. Therefore, I decided to make my own wedding gown from scratch. It became exactly what I wanted - a timeless, chic dress with that little extra something. When it was ready, it was love at first sight for me. And when I showed the dress to my friends and relatives, they all asked me where I got it from. That is when the idea of Amorée suddenly came to me.

"We want your "love at first sight" to be a "love at every sight."

Now, 10 years later, Amorée has become one of the most loved bridal brands in Finland. Our aim is to grow and join the international bridal market in the upcoming years, so that lovely brides from all over the world can have the chance to wear our Amorée wedding dresses on the most special day of their lives. We also want to remain a bridal brand known for the quality and timelessness of our collections. Our dresses are made in Finland in the most environmentally friendly way, and our goal is to create dresses that you are going to want to wear on your wedding day, but also on many other special days in your life. We want your "love at first sight" to be a "love at every sight" - now, and in the future.

With love,

Amelia Andersson, the founder of Amorée
